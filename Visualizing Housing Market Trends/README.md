# Visualizing Housing Market Trends: An Analysis of Sale Prices and Features using Tableau

##  Project Overview

The **Visualizing Housing Market Trends: An Analysis of Sale Prices and Features using Tableau** project focuses on analyzing residential housing data to identify the major factors influencing house prices and market trends. Using **Tableau**, interactive dashboards and visualizations are created to explore relationships between property characteristics such as house age, renovation history, number of bedrooms, bathrooms, floors, basement area, and sale price.

The project aims to provide meaningful insights into housing market behavior, enabling stakeholders to make informed decisions regarding property valuation, investment, and market analysis.

---

#  Objectives

* Analyze housing market trends using Tableau.
* Identify factors influencing house sale prices.
* Study the impact of renovations on property value.
* Compare property features such as bedrooms, bathrooms, and floors.
* Build an interactive dashboard for effective data exploration.
* Present insights through a Tableau Storyboard.
* Publish and integrate the dashboard into a Flask web application.

#  Worksheets & Story Points

---

## 📌 Sheet 1: Count of Houses

### Description

Displays the total number of houses available in the dataset, providing an overview of the data used for analysis.

### Story

This worksheet provides a summary of the dataset by displaying the total number of residential properties. It serves as the foundation for understanding the scale of the housing market analyzed in the project.

---

## 📌 Sheet 2: Average Sale Price

### Description

Shows the average selling price of all houses in the dataset.

### Story

This worksheet highlights the average market value of residential properties. It helps users understand the general pricing trend and serves as a benchmark for comparing housing features.

---

## 📌 Sheet 3: Basement Area Analysis

### Description

Displays the total basement area of all houses.

### Story

This worksheet analyzes the contribution of basement space across residential properties. It helps evaluate how additional basement area contributes to overall housing characteristics.

---

## 📌 Sheet 4: Total Sales by Years Since Renovation

### Description

Illustrates the relationship between sale prices and the number of years since renovation.

### Story

This visualization explores the impact of renovations on housing sales. It demonstrates how renovation history influences property value and helps identify trends associated with recently renovated homes.

---

## 📌 Sheet 5: Distribution of House Age by Renovation Status

### Description

Shows the distribution of houses based on age and renovation status using a pie chart.

### Story

This worksheet compares renovated and non-renovated houses, providing insight into the age distribution of properties and highlighting renovation patterns within the housing market.

---

## 📌 Sheet 6: House Age Distribution by Bathrooms, Bedrooms and Floors

### Description

Compares house age with the number of bathrooms, bedrooms, and floors.

### Story

This worksheet analyzes how important housing features vary across properties of different ages. It helps identify trends in residential design and supports better market understanding.
